How I work

Understanding patterns instead of assigning blame.

Counseling is not about making one person the guilty one. It is about understanding the shared pattern: What happens between you? What does it trigger in you? And what do you then do that may further intensify the situation?

I work directly, respectfully and with a focus on relationship. Difficult things may be spoken — not in order to hurt, but to come back into contact with yourself and with each other.

Frequently asked questions

What you can know beforehand

Does my partner have to come with me?

+

No. Relationship counseling can also be useful individually. Sometimes clarification begins when one person better understands what they are experiencing, what they need and what they want to change.

Is counseling also suitable for open or polyamorous relationships?

+

Yes. I support people in monogamous, open, polyamorous, queer and not clearly named relationship constellations. What matters is not the label, but what feels coherent, safe and responsible for you or for all of you.

Is this psychotherapy?

+

No. My offer is psychosocial counseling and does not replace psychotherapy or medical treatment. If it becomes clear that a different frame would be more suitable, I will address this transparently.

What happens in the introductory call?

+

We clarify your concern, the possible frame, first patterns and whether further work together would make sense. You do not have to know exactly what the solution is yet.

Is online counseling possible?

+

Yes, counseling is possible online. We clarify further details in the introductory call.
